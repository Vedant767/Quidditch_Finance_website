CMT Preparation Classes

CMT level 1 preparation class will last for 3 months

The classes will be conducted twice every week, mostly over weekends. Students with sharp grasping power & innate passion to learn & work will be considered for internship with us.

Around 60% of Level 1 candidates clear the examination.

The biggest issue is that candidates assume that, because they have been using Technical Analysis for years so this will be very easy exam for them. It’s not! Technical Analysis is a broad field of study with many deeper concepts. The CMT-1 exam is going to test your understanding of many of those concepts.

Each week is broken up into chapters of focus

To assist candidates with the CMT-1 exam, we are providing a live online course on all of the material that you need to know for the exam. Live sessions will range from 1- 2 hours depending on the complexity and length of the topics being covered.

WorkShop

One stop solution for getting started with the knowledge of trading and technical analysis.

Workshop is intended to be short & easy to understand, more of a joyful experience by putting things into right perspective rather than over-fueling of information which is freely available everywhere. Filled with interesting conversation of importance of sane & serene mind while trading. Connecting with like-minded people and fellow beginners. Students with sharp grasping power & innate passion to learn & work will be considered for internship with us.

What if you have questions or don’t understand something?

- We will create an online group of students including your instructor where you can ask questions.
- Once you register for the course, you’ll be added to the group.
- We’ll be monitoring that daily and will respond with answers to questions there.
